What We Offer

Warranty on Existing Cars

At Vinayaka Cars, we stand behind the quality of our vehicles. That's why we offer warranties on existing cars, providing you with added peace of mind and assurance in your purchase. Our warranties cover specified components, ensuring that you can drive with confidence knowing that your vehicle is protected.

Car Washing

Experience the ultimate in cleanliness and shine with our professional car washing services. Our skilled team uses industry-leading techniques and high-quality products to wash your car thoroughly, removing dirt, grime, and stains. Whether it's a quick wash or a deep cleaning, we'll leave your car sparkling inside and out.

Dry Cleaning Cars

Keep your car's interior looking pristine with our dry cleaning services. Our expert cleaners use specialized equipment and techniques to remove dirt, dust, and stains from upholstery, carpets, and other interior surfaces. Say goodbye to odors and unsightly marks, and enjoy a fresh, clean driving experience.

Car Polishing

Restore your car's exterior shine and luster with our professional car polishing services. Using premium polishes and buffers, our skilled technicians will eliminate surface imperfections, swirl marks, and scratches, leaving your car looking like new. Whether you want to enhance the appearance of your vehicle or protect its paintwork, our polishing services are the perfect solution.
